Questions answered

  • How did sales volume change across economic periods (2008 crash, pandemic, post-2022 cooling)?
  • When did “below assessed value” sales spike, and how did it normalize?
  • How did average housing prices shift across property types over time?
  • Do commercial assessed values align with sale prices across towns (2020–2024)?

Findings

What I did (short method)

  • Standardized date fields and fixed inconsistent year formatting (e.g., 0023 → 2023).
  • Filtered to housing categories for housing-specific trends; kept commercial separate for town comparison.
  • Grouped by year / town / property type to compute counts, shares, and average prices.
